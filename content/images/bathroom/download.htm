About Bella

Bathrooms

Based in Durham, Bella Bathrooms has been providing top quality bathrooms since 2005 at unbeatable prices. We specialise in designer products without the designer price tags, ranging from bathroom suites and plumbing essentials to everything in-between.

Bella Bathrooms was founded by James Chapman, a qualified plumber with extensive experience in installing bathrooms. After working on bathrooms with a mixed scale of budgets, he decided to make it his mission to make sure everyone in the UK has access to affordable, premium quality bathroom fittings and furniture.

We’re an honest company and if we wouldn’t put something in our own home bathrooms, we won’t stock it – it’s as simple as that! The bathroom is one of the most important rooms in the home – a place to relax and unwind, or refresh and feel invigorated. Renovating a bathroom or adding a new one to a property is one of the quickest ways to add value to a home, which shows how much we appreciate our restrooms. Bella Bathrooms understands the significance of the contemporary bathroom, and we believe you shouldn’t have to compromise on style or budget.
